### 7. Product Codes & International Listings

**Code Types:**
- **EAN:** 13-digit codes (European, also used in Canada)
- **UPC:** 12-digit codes (US standard)
- Best Way has both for most products

**Technical Considerations:**
- Typically EAN = UPC with leading zero
- Best Way's codes have different leading digits (non-standard)
- Same product may have different codes in different markets
- Amazon treats different codes as separate listings
- **Implication:** Need to check both codes for existing listings
- **Opportunity:** Copy existing global listing data to Canadian listing

**Greg's Preference:**
- Uses EAN codes even in US for consistency
- Easier for packaging and operations
- Retailer systems may dictate requirements (US prefers UPC)

## Appendix: Terminology Reference

- **3PL:** Third-Party Logistics provider
- **DDP:** Delivered Duty Paid (seller pays all shipping and customs)
- **FOB:** Free On Board (buyer arranges shipping from port)
- **LCL:** Less than Container Load
- **EAN:** European Article Number (13 digits)
- **UPC:** Universal Product Code (12 digits)
- **2P:** Second-Party (wholesale to retailer who resells)
- **3P:** Third-Party (marketplace seller)
- **FBA:** Fulfillment by Amazon
- **CARM:** Canadian Assessment and Revenue Management
- **LLM:** Large Language Model
- **SEO:** Search Engine Optimization
- **SKU:** Stock Keeping Unit
