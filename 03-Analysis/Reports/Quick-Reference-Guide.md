# Product Validation Quick Reference Guide

## Validation Methodology Summary

### 6-Factor Scoring Framework

1. **Margin After All Fees** (5 = >35%, 1 = <10%)
2. **Competition & Amazon Retail Risk** (5 = <20 sellers, no Amazon Retail, 1 = Amazon Retail owns buy box)
3. **Seasonality** (5 = year-round, 1 = single-season)
4. **Cube / Logistics Complexity** (5 = small/lightweight, 1 = oversized/fragile)
5. **Insurance & Compliance Risk** (5 = low risk, 1 = prohibitive costs)
6. **Velocity vs Inventory Risk** (5 = high BSR <5K, 1 = no sales rank data)

### Scoring Thresholds

- **24-30 points:** GO (prioritize for first launch)
- **18-23 points:** HOLD (needs tweaks or more data)
- **12-17 points:** CAUTION (strategic only)
- **<12 points:** KILL (do not order)

## Key Metrics to Monitor Post-Launch

### Weekly Metrics
- Sales velocity (units/day)
- ACOS (Advertising Cost of Sales)
- Net margin (actual vs projected)
- Inventory levels
- BSR trends

### Monthly Metrics
- Total revenue
- Net profit
- Inventory turnover
- Storage fees
- Return rate

### Red Flags Checklist

#### Immediate Action Required
- [ ] Net margin drops below 10%
- [ ] Amazon Retail enters and owns buy box
- [ ] Inventory sitting >90 days (storage fees accumulating)
- [ ] ACOS >30% consistently
- [ ] Return rate >5%

#### Review and Adjust
- [ ] Sales velocity declining
- [ ] BSR worsening
- [ ] Competitor count increasing significantly
- [ ] Price compression (race to bottom)
- [ ] Review sentiment declining

## DataDive Quick Reference

### Essential Features
1. **Master Keyword List (MKL)**
   - Total search volume
   - Keyword count (need >30)
   - Competitor rankings

2. **Roots Analysis**
   - Main keyword roots (need >3)
   - Search volume distribution

3. **Competitor Analysis**
   - Top 10 competitors
   - Strength ratings (Weak/Strong/Very Strong)
   - Market concentration

4. **Product Scorecard**
   - 6-factor risk assessment
   - Overall risk score

### DataDive Red Flags
- <30 relevant keywords
- <3 main keyword roots
- <2 strong competitors
- Amazon Retail presence
- High risk score on scorecard

## Common Product Categories - Risk Profiles

### Low Risk (Generally)
- Small, lightweight products
- Year-round demand
- Low insurance requirements
- Established market with proven demand

### High Risk (Generally)
- High cube products (pool floats, lounges)
- Heavy seasonality (summer products)
- High insurance costs (trampolines, pools)
- New products with no sales rank
- Amazon Retail active in category

---

## Decision Framework

### When to GO
- Score 24-30
- Validated margins >25%
- Low Amazon Retail risk
- Manageable competition
- Year-round or light seasonality

### When to HOLD
- Score 18-23
- Needs margin validation
- Requires competitor research
- Needs seasonality confirmation
- Insurance quotes pending

### When to KILL
- Score <12
- Multiple red flags
- Prohibitive costs (insurance, storage)
- Amazon Retail dominant
- No proven demand
